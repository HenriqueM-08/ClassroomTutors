Setting up your booking calendar

1. Open Google Calendar

2. Click "Create" -> "Appointment schedule" on the top left-hand corner.

3. Enter appointment title as "(Your Name) Tutoring" and select 30-minute appointment durations.

4. Choose your availability for each day.

5. Click "Next".

6. Add a short description about your tutoring service.

7. Under "Booking Form", add three extra required items:

"Year Group",

"Subject Needed",

"Description of Help Needed".

8. Click "Save".

9. Once saved, click "Open booking page".

10. Copy the URL and email it to classroomtutorshelp@gmail.com, including your full name.
