Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tutoring session?

Simply browse our tutor profiles, select a tutor that matches your needs, and click "Book Session." You'll be guided through the scheduling process.

What qualifications do your tutors have?

All tutors have studied and passed their subject areas. Their grades and achievements are displayed on their profiles.

Are tutoring sessions free?

Yes! All tutoring sessions are free of charge. Our platform connects students with tutors who volunteer their time to help others learn.

Do you offer both online and in-person tutoring?

Yes! Our platform supports both online and in-school tutoring sessions, depending on tutor availability and your preference. For in-person tutoring, the student must be in secondary (Y7-Y12).

How do I become a tutor on your platform?

Visit our "Become a Tutor" page to start the application process. You'll need to provide information about yourself and your grades. Our team will review your application within 3-5 business days.

What subjects do you offer tutoring for?

We offer tutoring in over 25 subjects including Mathematics, Sciences, English, History, Geography, Computer Science, and SAT preparation. If you don't see your subject listed, contact us!
